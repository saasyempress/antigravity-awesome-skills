# 30_review

Purpose:
This folder is the human review and decision layer.

What enters this folder:
- Machine generated drafts from 05_autorun_outputs
- Machine generated quality reviews
- Machine generated briefs requiring approval

What happens here:
- Human review
- Human edits (optional)
- Human approval or rejection

Rules:
- Nothing moves forward without human approval
- Human edits are allowed here
- This folder operates on human time, not system time

Outcomes:
- Approved content moves to 40_approved
- Rejected content is archived or ignored
